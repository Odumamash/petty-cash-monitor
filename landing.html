Features

Everything you need to
manage petty cash

Built specifically for the Oasis Health workflow — no complex setup, no subscriptions.

📊

Live Dashboard

5 summary cards showing total spend, top category, daily average, drugs total, and IP meals at a glance.

📁

Excel Import

Upload your OPENFLOAT_UTILIZATION_REPORT.xlsx directly. The dashboard reads your categories and data automatically.

🥧

Visual Charts

Donut chart by category and daily bar chart give you an instant visual breakdown of where money is going.

✏️

Add & Edit Expenses

Add new transactions directly in the dashboard. Reclassify any expense category with a single dropdown click.

📧

Weekly Email Report

One click generates a pre-filled email to mashudoduma@gmail.com with total spend, category breakdown, and top expenses.

🔍

Search & Sort

Filter transactions by description or payee. Sort by date, payee, or amount. Delete any incorrect entry instantly.
